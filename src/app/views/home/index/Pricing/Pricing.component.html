Weekly payouts fee and anytime payouts .

We send money weekly from your TYBO account to your bank account for free.

Then each time you request money to be sent from your TYBO account to your bank account, we charge a payout fee of R15 excluding VAT, regardless of the value of the payout.

Customer refunds .

You can refund your customers who paid using TYBO. Refunds can be processed by contacting our Support Team, we charge a refund fee of R5.
